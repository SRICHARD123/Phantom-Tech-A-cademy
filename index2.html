About Us

Shaping Africa’s future tech leaders

Phantom Tech Academy is a modern online tech training platform built to equip students, graduates, and young professionals with the digital skills needed to thrive in today’s competitive tech-driven world

We combine live mentorship, self-paced learning, hands-on projects, and career guidance to deliver a complete learning experience. Our programs are designed by industry experts to match global standards while being flexible and accessible from anywhere
